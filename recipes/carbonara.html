The Amazing Creamy Chicken Carbonara Inspired by Odin!

Whip up this super easy to make chicken carbonara in 15 minutes

It's an easy family take on the traditional chicken carbonara recipe.

The Secret Carbonara

    INGREDIENTS
  • 500g Coles Brand Penne
  • 1 tbsp Red Island Australian Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 500g Coles RSPCA Approved Chicken Breast Fillets, cut into 2cm pieces
    • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 cup (250ml) Campbell's Real Stock Chicken
  • 2 Coles Brand Australian Free Range Egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup (185ml) thickened cream
  • 1 tbsp Coles Cornflour
  • 1/2 cup (40g) parmesan, finely grated
  • 2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, chopped
    Steps
  • 1. Cook the Coles Brand Penne in a saucepan of boiling water following packet directions. Drain.
  • 2. Meanwhile, heat the Red Island Australian Extra Virgin Olive Oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add chicken and cook for 4 mins, turning, until golden and cooked through. Transfer to a plate. Add bacon to the pan and cook, stirring, for 2 mins or until golden and starting to crisp.
  • 3. Whisk garlic, Campbell's Real Stock Chicken, egg yolks, cream, cornflour and half the parmesan in a bowl. Season. Return the chicken to the pan over low heat. Add the egg mixture and pasta. Cook, tossing, for 1-2 mins or until pasta is coated and the sauce thickens. Top with parsley and remaining parmesan.
