Delicious Spaghetti Sauce Inspired by Odin!

The onion is unsauteed, the sauce simmers for less than an hour, and there's a lot of butter. It's the easiest and, most likely, best red sauce you'll ever make at home. Here's what to do:

The Secret Spaghetti Sauce

    INGREDIENTS
  • 1 (28-oz.) can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 5 tbsp. butter
  • 1 onion, cut in half
  • 2 sprigs basil
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt
    Steps
  1. Combine everything, including a pinch of salt, in a saucepan. Simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally and breaking up the tomatoes, for about 45 minutes, until it reaches the consistency of pasta sauce.
  2. Remove the onion, basil, and garlic and serve with spaghetti.
  3. CONSUME!
